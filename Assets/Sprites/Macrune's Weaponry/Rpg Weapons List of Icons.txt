List of Weapons that are in this pack

*Note:
Im using a rows and columns as coordinates (columns, rows) with the 16x16 spritesheet as reference.
The top most left as (1, 1) and the bottom most right as (28, 28).*

In this weapon pack, I include some color variations for the normal weapons representing different ore/materials used in the weapon.
Light Brown: Copper
Grey: Iron
White: Silver
Yellow: Gold
Blue: Cobalt
Green: Mythril
Purple: Amethyst
Black: Steel

Of course, you can change the name of the materials used depending on your preferences.
I add these names as a guide to help you understand what I'm making.
